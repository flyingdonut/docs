## Real-Time Agile Collaboration, Simplified

Flying Donut is a powerful, simple, and effective online Scrum and Kanban tool designed from the ground up as a **real-time agile collaboration platform**. Built for transparency and seamless communication, it empowers distributed, remote, and co-located teams to work together effortlessly.

Simplicity is at the core of Flying Donut's design. We believe teams should **be agile**, not just practice agile. By stripping away complexity and focusing on what matters, Flying Donut delivers a real-time interface that makes collaboration fluid and natural.
