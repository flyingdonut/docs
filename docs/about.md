# About Flying Donut
{: .no_toc .fs-9 }

## What is Flying Donut?

Flying Donut is a powerful, simple, effective, collaborative online Scrum and Kanban tool, 
set out, from the start, to be a real-time agile collaboration tool, to allow transparency 
and communication across distributed, remote or co-located teams. 

Simplicity is the core idea underlying the design of Flying Donut, allowing teams to be agile and not just practice 
agile. It goes back to the basics of agile, with a real-time interface that makes collaboration so fluid, effortlessly.

## Key Features

### Real-Time Collaboration
Flying Donut provides instant updates across all team members. When any team member modifies an entry, 
everyone's view is immediately updated, eliminating the need for constant refresh or email notifications.

### Scrum and Kanban Support
Whether your team prefers Scrum methodology with sprints and phases, or Kanban-style continuous flow, 
Flying Donut supports both approaches seamlessly.

### Project Management Made Simple
- Break down projects into manageable phases with clear deadlines
- Organize tasks into customizable categories
- Track progress visually with intuitive boards
- Collaborate effortlessly with local and remote team members

### Beyond Software Development
While Flying Donut excels at managing software development projects, it's designed to handle any type of project. 
From organizing house moves to planning events, any project can benefit from agile-style project management.

## Core Benefits

**Improved Communication**: Foster better communication between team members while eliminating unnecessary email traffic.

**Zero Learning Curve**: Flying Donut is intuitive and easy to learn. Get started immediately without lengthy training sessions.

**Instant Actions**: Every action is instantaneous, keeping your team's workflow smooth and uninterrupted.

**Flexible Visibility**: Choose between private projects for sensitive work or public projects for open collaboration.

**Distributed Team Support**: Perfect for remote, distributed, or co-located teams with real-time synchronization.

## Who Uses Flying Donut?

Flying Donut is ideal for:
- Software development teams practicing Scrum or Kanban
- Project managers coordinating distributed teams
- Agile practitioners seeking simplicity over complexity
- Teams of any size looking for transparent collaboration tools
- Anyone managing projects that benefit from iterative approaches
